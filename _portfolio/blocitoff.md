## Explanation

One of the chief things I do is create to do lists. One frustration I have is that when completing a task I need to delete the task through a slow highlight and destroy. However, with this tool I have created a to do list that removes items after a duration or upon completion. Because as we all know sometimes things just don't get done and rather then punish ourselves by looking over old material, its better to just let the task disappear. With this application you can do just that.
